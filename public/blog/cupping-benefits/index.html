Cupping Benefits

June 6, 2025 · 1 min read

Cupping therapy is a traditional healing technique used to improve circulation and relieve muscle tension.

What Is Cupping Therapy?

Cupping therapy uses suction cups placed on the skin to stimulate blood flow and support healing.

Benefits of Cupping

  • Reduces muscle tension
  • Improves circulation
  • Supports recovery
  • Promotes relaxation
